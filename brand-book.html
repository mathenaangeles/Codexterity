03 · Color

Near-black canvas. Volt earns attention.

The site is a near-black system with white type. Volt is the single call-to-action color; mint, aqua, and cobalt appear as data accents and ambient glows. Accents are never fields, with one exception: volt fills CTAs, selected chips, and the active sweep row.

04 · Typography

Sora for statements. Inter for speed. Mono for machinery.

Garet is the licensed display face for offline collateral; on the web it is Sora (600–800). Body is Inter. JetBrains Mono carries the system voice: eyebrows, statuses, meta, receipts, and anything that should feel like the machine talking.

Do

Sentence case everywhere, including headlines. Statuses capitalize the first letter: "Queued", "Done", "Working...".

Don't

05 · Voice

The engineer who fixed it, not the one who sold it.

Short, concrete, benefit-first. Copy names the task, then removes it. Product surfaces (demos, chips, statuses, receipts) speak in full sentences with periods.

RuleExample from the site
Lead with the outcome"Your busywork gets done while you sleep."
Numbers over adjectives"142 runs this week, 0 errors." · "Resolved in 8 seconds at 2:04 AM."
Full sentences in product UI"Refunded the order and replied to Sam." not "refund sent"
Plain qualification"If you do it more than twice a week, it can probably run itself."
Honest automationDemos always show one task escalated to a human ("Marked for Review").
Never

Em dashes. Use commas, periods, or a colon instead.

Never

Hype vocabulary: revolutionary, cutting-edge, supercharge, unleash, 10x.

Never

Strings of tiny "a · b · c" meta fragments as user-facing copy.

07 · Motion

Calm by default. Alive on touch.

One easing curve everywhere: cubic-bezier(.16,.84,.28,1). Motion explains state; it never decorates. Everything respects prefers-reduced-motion.

LayerBehavior
Smooth scrollLenis, lerp 0.115, synced to GSAP ScrollTrigger; anchors offset for the fixed nav.
RevealsRise-and-fade (24px, 0.8–0.9s), word-stagger on headlines. Triggered once per element.
HeroCanvas dot-wave plane in the brand ramp; ambient ripple plus a local lift under the cursor. Typewriter rotator cycles the keyword.
Scroll depthGlows drift on data-parallax; the Statement section fills word-by-word as you read through it.
HoverSpotlight follows the pointer on cards; magnetic CTAs; sweep fills; logo lift + name reveal on the stack sheet.
DemosCSS keyframes on a shared 8s clock; statuses flip fully (out, then in, never a double-exposure crossfade).
